REB00T by Rewired & Rising

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 30, 2026

1. Overview

REB00T is designed to support habit recovery with private tracking, reminders, educational content, and intervention tools. By default, your recovery data is stored on your device.

If you enable optional iCloud sync inside the app, Apple may sync certain app data across devices connected to your iCloud account.

2. Information The App Stores

The app may store the following information so it can function correctly:

  • Onboarding answers and personalization inputs
  • Sobriety date, streak history, and milestone progress
  • Urge logs, check-ins, and protocol usage history
  • Firewall and blocker-related setup state
  • Notification preferences and reminder scheduling data
  • Subscription entitlement status needed to unlock premium access

3. Subscription And Payment Data

Purchases are processed by Apple through your Apple ID. REB00T does not directly collect or store your full payment card information.

REB00T uses RevenueCat to help validate purchases, restore access, and determine whether premium entitlement is active on your device.

4. How Information Is Used

Your information is used to:

  • Save recovery progress and personalize the in-app experience
  • Schedule reminders and notification-based support when you enable them
  • Unlock and restore premium access
  • Support optional iCloud sync and data export/delete controls

5. What Stays Private

Recovery journal content, urge logs, check-ins, and local progress data are intended to remain on your device unless you choose to sync through Apple services such as iCloud. The app is designed so that this recovery data is not transmitted to Rewired & Rising servers as part of normal app use.

6. Third-Party Services

REB00T depends on Apple platform services and may rely on:

  • Apple for App Store billing, account management, notifications, and optional iCloud sync
  • RevenueCat for subscription entitlement and restore handling

These providers may process limited information according to their own privacy practices.

7. Your Controls

Inside the app, you can:

  • Export your data
  • Delete your data
  • Disable optional iCloud sync
  • Change notification permissions in iOS Settings
  • Manage subscriptions through your Apple account

8. Data Retention

Data stored locally remains on your device until you delete it, remove the app, or choose the in-app delete option. If optional iCloud sync is enabled, synced data may remain in Apple-managed storage until removed there as well.

9. Children

REB00T is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, stop using the app on that device and remove the stored data.

10. Changes To This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The current version will be posted at this page with an updated effective date.
