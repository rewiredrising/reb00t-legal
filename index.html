REB00T by Rewired & Rising

Quit porn. Reclaim your mind.

REB00T is an iPhone recovery app built to help users interrupt compulsive porn use with private tracking, blocking support, relapse prevention tools, and a more structured reset.

What It Does

Built for weak-window protection

REB00T combines guided onboarding, relapse interruption tools, and blocker setup designed to reduce access during high-risk moments.

Private By Design

Recovery data stays local by default

Onboarding answers, logs, streak data, and recovery progress are designed to stay on the device unless the user explicitly enables Apple services such as iCloud sync.

Core Focus

A structured reset, not generic motivation

The app experience centers on interruption, blocker support, personal accountability, and week-one recovery structure rather than passive content consumption.

  • Guided onboarding and weak-window analysis
  • Protocol and relapse interruption tools
  • Blocker and Screen Time support
  • Private logs, check-ins, and progress tracking
